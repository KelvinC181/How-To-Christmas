Christmas Activities in the UK

Choose an activity category to find fun things to do this festive season!

Christmas Markets

Edinburgh

Edinburgh's winter festivals, which combine Christmas and Hogmanay from November to January, are among the best times to visit the city. You can pick and choose from many events and things to do. Edinburgh Christmas Market is one of the most popular events, and in this guide, I list the confirmed dates for 2024 with opening times and locations.

Manchester

Now in their 26th year, Manchester's Christmas Markets have become legendary, drawing millions to the city centre. With over 200 stalls spread across nine locations, visitors can enjoy seasonal treats, mulled wine, and a wonderful festive atmosphere.

Bath

The Bath Christmas Market will return to the heart of Bath stretching across the historic city centre. As you wander through the cobblestone streets, you'll be immersed in the festive ambiance, while discovering an array of unique handmade gifts from independent makers from Bath and beyond.

Illuminations

Glow Wild

This award-winning light trail transforms Wakehurst's botanical gardens into a magical winter wonderland with over a thousand light installations. Highlights include a towering sequoia tree and giant animal lanterns. It’s a must-see for art lovers and nature enthusiasts alike​

Christmas at Bedgebury

The Bedgebury National Pinetum & Forest offers a stunning light display along its forest trails. Visitors can enjoy vibrant light displays, including neon gallopers and sparkling tunnels, set against the backdrop of an ancient forest

Northern Lights

The Northern Lights in Newcastle is an annual light festival featuring stunning art installations and light projections across the city. This free event transforms public spaces and landmarks with interactive displays, offering a unique experience for both locals and visitors.

Ice Rinks

Natural History Museum Ice Rink

The Natural History Museum Ice Rink in London is a magical winter destination set against the backdrop of the iconic museum's stunning architecture. This outdoor rink features sparkling lights and a festive atmosphere, making it the perfect place for skating with friends and family. Open from mid-November to early January, it’s a must-see holiday attraction. Whether you're gliding across the ice or enjoying a warm drink from the café, the rink provides an unforgettable experience for all ages​​

Somerset House Ice Rink

The Somerset House Ice Rink in London offers a magical skating experience in one of the capital’s most iconic locations. Nestled within the stunning courtyard of Somerset House, the rink is beautifully framed by the grandeur of the historic building and the twinkling festive lights. Open from November through January, this ice rink is perfect for all ages and skill levels, with a charming atmosphere enhanced by seasonal events, a Christmas shop, and a stylish skate lounge for warming up. It’s a must-visit for those looking for a sophisticated and memorable skating experience in London.

Skate Manchester

Skate Manchester offers a magical winter experience in the heart of the city. Set in the picturesque Cathedral Gardens, this seasonal ice rink provides a stunning backdrop of the cityscape, making it perfect for skaters of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're gliding around with friends or enjoying the festive atmosphere, the rink is covered, so you can skate no matter the weather. The rink is open daily, offering flexible 45-minute sessions, and skate hire is included. Plus, after skating, you can warm up with delicious treats from the nearby Christmas market​

Christmas Experiences

Hyde Park Winter Wonderland

Hyde Park Winter Wonderland, London is a festive spectacular in the heart of the city, offering ice skating, a giant Ferris wheel, a traditional fairground, and Christmas markets. With live performances, seasonal treats, and unique shopping opportunities, it’s the perfect place to experience the magic of Christmas. Whether you're skating, riding, or enjoying festive food, there's something for everyone at this holiday wonderland.​

12 Days of Christmas Belvoir Castle

The "Twelve Days of Christmas" at Belvoir Castle brings the festive carol to life with stunning rooms filled with enchanting displays. Each room represents a verse from the song, from lords a-leaping to golden rings. The castle is adorned with beautiful trees and holiday decor, creating a magical atmosphere. Visitors can also enjoy afternoon tea and shop for unique gifts. The event runs through December 31st

Carols in the Cloister

"Carols in the Cloister" is a festive event at Gloucester Cathedral where visitors can enjoy the beauty of Christmas carols performed in the historic cloisters. The cathedral's stunning surroundings provide a perfect backdrop for a magical holiday experience. The event typically features local choirs singing traditional carols, creating a warm and joyous atmosphere. It's a wonderful way to get into the Christmas spirit while experiencing the cathedral's architectural grandeur. The event usually runs during the Christmas season, with exact dates available on the cathedral's official website.

Santa Visits

Santa's Winter Wonderland at Snowdome

Santa's Winter Wonderland at SnowDome is a magical festive experience where families can enjoy real snow activities, including sliding, sledging, and exploring a sparkling snow trail. Meet Santa and his cheerful elves, watch an entertaining live stage show, and visit the enchanting Christmas Village for festive treats and shopping. Packed with holiday cheer, it’s the perfect way to create unforgettable Christmas memories! ​
