Hyde Park Winter Wonderland

Hyde Park Winter Wonderland, London is a festive spectacular in the heart of the city, offering ice skating, a giant Ferris wheel, a traditional fairground, and Christmas markets. With live performances, seasonal treats, and unique shopping opportunities, it’s the perfect place to experience the magic of Christmas. Whether you're skating, riding, or enjoying festive food, there's something for everyone at this holiday wonderland. ​​
