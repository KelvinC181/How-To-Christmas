Carols in the Cloister

"Carols in the Cloister" is a festive event at Gloucester Cathedral where visitors can enjoy the beauty of Christmas carols performed in the historic cloisters. The cathedral's stunning surroundings provide a perfect backdrop for a magical holiday experience. The event typically features local choirs singing traditional carols, creating a warm and joyous atmosphere. It's a wonderful way to get into the Christmas spirit while experiencing the cathedral's architectural grandeur. The event usually runs during the Christmas season, with exact dates available on the cathedral's official website. ​​
