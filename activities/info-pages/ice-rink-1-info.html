Natural History Museum Ice Rink

The Natural History Museum Ice Rink in London is a magical winter destination set against the backdrop of the iconic museum's stunning architecture. This outdoor rink features sparkling lights and a festive atmosphere, making it the perfect place for skating with friends and family. Open from mid-November to early January, it’s a must-see holiday attraction. Whether you're gliding across the ice or enjoying a warm drink from the café, the rink provides an unforgettable experience for all ages​​
