Skate Manchester

Skate Manchester offers a magical winter experience in the heart of the city. Set in the picturesque Cathedral Gardens, this seasonal ice rink provides a stunning backdrop of the cityscape, making it perfect for skaters of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're gliding around with friends or enjoying the festive atmosphere, the rink is covered, so you can skate no matter the weather. The rink is open daily, offering flexible 45-minute sessions, and skate hire is included. Plus, after skating, you can warm up with delicious treats from the nearby Christmas market​ ​​
