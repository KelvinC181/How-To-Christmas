Manchester Christmas Market

Now in their 26th year, Manchester's Christmas Markets have become legendary, drawing millions to the city centre. With over 200 stalls spread across nine locations, visitors can enjoy seasonal treats, mulled wine, and a wonderful festive atmosphere. Whether you're after handmade gifts, craft items, or delicious food, the markets cater to all tastes and ages.
