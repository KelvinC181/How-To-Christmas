Santa's Special Trains - Epping Ongar Railway

Santa's Special Trains at the Epping Ongar Railway offer a magical Christmas experience for families Guests begin their journey with a ride on a vintage London bus from Epping Underground station or Ongar, leading to North Weald Station. There, you board Santa's Special Train for a festive return trip through the scenic Epping Forest. Children will meet Santa on the train, receive gifts, and enjoy entertainment. The event also features festive food stalls and live music at the station.
